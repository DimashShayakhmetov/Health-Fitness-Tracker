🌟 Our Story

About the founder

I’m Dimash, a founder of the Health&Fitness Tracking platform. My journey began at 12 years old, practicing pull-ups on rusty playground bars in Astana. By 16, I started to participate in street workout competitions. In 2022, after winning Kazakhstan’s National Functional Fitness Championship, I realized health isn’t just about training, it's about nutrition, healthy habits, accessibility, and community.

Health & Fitness Tracker was born from a simple idea: What if technology could be used in collaboration with a trainer in order to achieve best results.

🧬 Core Values

  • 🔥 Human-Centric Design: The programs have been tested with real users—from beginners to elite athletes.
  • 🌱 Sustainable Progress: Firstly we focus on habits, not extremes.
  • 🤝 Community First: Your progress inspires a movement.
